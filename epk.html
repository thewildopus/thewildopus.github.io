The Wild Opus

Electronic pop music in Austin, TX

Bio

Pushing the boundaries of pop, the Wild Opus creates a unique style lined with elements of electronic, pop, trip-hop, and avant-garde. This multidisciplinary project combine otherworldy electronic pop music with fantastical visuals and performance. The recent music video 'With the Crocodiles' includes psychedelic imagery and artistic direction, combining the work of 20 different contributors in Austin, Texas.

The Wild Opus is always doing something new, whether creating interactive visuals, theatrical performances, acoustic sets, or unexpected collaborations. Drawing listeners in with catchy melodies, groovy basslines, they are sure to walk away with a memorable experience.
